About

Welcome to Flora Andriany Photography


A mother of two who loves travelling and taking pictures.

I have lived all over the world in countries such as the United States, Japan, Dubai, Indonesia and Thailand. For the past 6 years, I've resided in Paris, France.

Photography is my passion. I have been doing it for more than a decade. Before Flora Andriany Photography, I previously worked under the name Oyamoto. I'm always striving to deliver my best work. I love taking portraits, family photos, travel photos, etc.

Go to the "Book" page if you would want to have your photo taken or someone to cover the photography of your event.
